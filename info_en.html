About TirzeCalc.de

What is TirzeCalc.de?

TirzeCalc.de is a free online calculator for calculating the required injection steps (so-called "clicks") for Tirzepatide pens like Mounjaro and KwikPen.

Features

  • Support for all common pen strengths (2.5mg to 15mg)
  • Precise calculation in 1.25mg increments
  • Easy to use without registration
  • Completely free and ad-free
  • Optimized for desktop and mobile
  • Dark/Light mode support
  • Multilingual (German/English)

How does it work?

  1. Select your pen strength
  2. Choose the desired dose using the slider
  3. The calculator shows you the required number of clicks

Important Notice

This calculator is for informational purposes only. Always consult your doctor or pharmacist regarding the correct dosage and use of your medications.

Technical Details

TirzeCalc.de was developed with modern web technologies and is completely client-side. This means your data is not transmitted to servers and remains completely private.
