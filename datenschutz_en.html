Privacy Policy

General Information

The following information provides a simple overview of what happens to your personal data when you visit this website. Personal data is any data with which you can be personally identified.

Data Collection on this Website

No active data collection:
No personal data is actively collected on TirzeCalc.de. No forms, registrations, or contact forms are offered.

No Cookies:
This website does not use cookies. No tracking technologies such as Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar services are used.

No external services:
No data is transferred to third-party providers. All calculations are performed locally in your browser.

Server Log Files

The provider of the pages automatically collects and stores information in so-called server log files, which your browser automatically transmits to us. These are:

  • Browser type and browser version
  • Operating system used
  • Referrer URL
  • Hostname of the accessing computer
  • Time of the server request
  • IP address

This data is not merged with other data sources.

Your Rights

You have the right at any time to receive free information about the origin, recipient, and purpose of your stored personal data. You also have a right to request the correction, blocking, or deletion of this data.
